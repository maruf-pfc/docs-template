# Banana - The Energy Booster

Bananas are a convenient and energy-rich fruit, making them a favorite snack for all ages.

## Benefits of Bananas

- High in potassium, supporting heart health.
- Provides instant energy and aids muscle recovery.
- Supports digestive health with natural fiber.

## Ways to Enjoy

- Eat fresh as a snack.
- Blend into smoothies.
- Use in baking for natural sweetness.
