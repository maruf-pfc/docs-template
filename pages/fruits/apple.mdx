# Apple - A Nutritious Fruit

Apples are one of the most popular and nutritious fruits, known for their crisp texture and sweet taste.

## Benefits of Apples

- Rich in fiber, aiding digestion.
- Packed with antioxidants that promote heart health.
- Helps regulate blood sugar levels.

## Popular Varieties

- **Fuji**: Sweet and juicy.
- **Granny Smith**: Tart and great for baking.
- **Honeycrisp**: Crisp and refreshing.
